Nike

Who We Are

Nike is one of the world’s leading sports brands, known for its innovative athletic footwear, apparel, and equipment. Founded in 1964, Nike inspires athletes around the globe with its mission: "To bring inspiration and innovation to every athlete in the world."

Our Mission

Nike believes that everyone is an athlete. The company focuses on performance, sustainability, and innovation to help people push their limits and achieve their goals.

Our Values

Innovation, sustainability, equality, and community impact are core to Nike’s identity. Through sports, Nike aims to create positive change worldwide.
